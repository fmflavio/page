User Information Security

We follow generally accepted industry standards and maintain reasonable safeguards to ensure the security, integrity and privacy of the information we hold. As a further security measure, we have implemented the following technical and administrative safeguards to ensure that no personal information of users we process is lost, leaked or damaged:

Improve network security - We implement various technical measures to prevent the leakage of your personal information through unauthorized access to its network through hacker attacks, computer viruses, etc., and continuously monitor network access. Additionally, to secure communications between its servers and the database, we employ strong encryption.

Conduct security training and designate a privacy officer - We regularly conduct security training for our employees on protecting your personal information. In addition, we have appointed a Privacy Officer to develop expertise in handling your personal information and minimise the risk of information leakage.

Restricted Access and Storage - Locations where personal information is processed and stored have been designated as secure areas and access is restricted to those with the necessary permissions. Documents, storage media and electronic records containing personal information are stored in locked locations or on computers with limited access.
